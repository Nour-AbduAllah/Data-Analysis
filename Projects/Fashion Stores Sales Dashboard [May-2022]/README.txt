This is a dashboard for fashion strors  data analytics projects.
	This projects visualises how are the stores sales are going per Chain, Months, State, and Category.

Our conclusions from this project are:
	-"Fashions Direct" chain has the most sales by 70.7%, instead the "Next Look" chain has only 29.3% total sales.
	-Total Sales for "Fashions Direct" are very higher than "Next Look" monthly.
	-Total sales in NSW state is the higher sales by $19,859,788.
	-The most trending category that gain sales is "Home" for "Fashions Direct" store by $6,758,157.
